About Us

Save Water

The Suvio Water System regulates the water used over the different areas of your lawn. The system makes sure that the censored areas of the lawn are not under watered or over watered by signaling the corresponding sprinkler heads to turn on whenever the moisture in that area is low. It also keeps you from over watering your lawn by turning off the corresponding sprinkler heads that sense a break in the watering limit.

Save Money

With Suvio Water system you save money. The system keeps you from over watering areas of your lawn. The system will shut off the sprinkler heads that correspond to the sensors when it senses your lawn has enough water. So by not wastefully over using water, you save money on your water bill.
